## Iron rules (learned the expensive way)
1. VERIFY FROM THE DATABASE, never from a status banner. Banners lie; the
   positions table doesn't.
2. Nothing deploys without a backtest number from YOUR OWN book attached.
3. One change at a time, then freeze. Config thrash makes your data unreadable.
4. Provisional/fast verdicts may size stakes ONLY. Money, P&L, and guardrails
   settle on Polymarket's on-chain resolution and nothing else.
5. Every bot patch: backup the file, syntax-check, deploy, restart, verify a
   live trade. No exceptions at 2am.
6. Internal balance drifts. Recalibrate against the authenticated CLOB cash
   number whenever floor accuracy matters.
7. Losses get explained, not avenged. If you can't explain a loss from data,
   that's the alarm — not a reason to change settings.
